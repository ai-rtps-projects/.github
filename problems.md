# 🤖 Chatbot Problem Statements

## 1. Customer Support Automation
**Problem:** Companies receive thousands of customer queries daily, causing long waiting times.  
**Solution:** A chatbot automatically answers FAQs like order status, refund policies, and product details.

## 2. Online Shopping Assistance
**Problem:** Customers need help finding or comparing products.  
**Solution:** Chatbots recommend products and answer queries based on user preferences.

## 3. Banking and Financial Services
**Problem:** Banks handle many routine queries about accounts and transactions.  
**Solution:** Chatbots provide instant responses and guide users through banking services.

## 4. Healthcare Appointment Scheduling
**Problem:** Hospitals struggle to manage appointments manually.  
**Solution:** Chatbots help book appointments, check availability, and provide health tips.

## 5. Education and Student Support
**Problem:** Students frequently ask about schedules, assignments, and exams.  
**Solution:** Chatbots provide instant academic support and information.

## 6. Travel and Hotel Booking
**Problem:** Travel agencies receive many booking-related inquiries.  
**Solution:** Chatbots assist with flight search, hotel booking, and travel updates.

## 7. Technical Support
**Problem:** Users face technical issues and need quick solutions.  
**Solution:** Chatbots provide step-by-step troubleshooting guidance.

## 8. Food Ordering Systems
**Problem:** Restaurants struggle during peak hours.  
**Solution:** Chatbots handle orders, suggest items, and process payments.

## 9. HR and Employee Support
**Problem:** Employees repeatedly ask HR-related questions.  
**Solution:** Chatbots automate HR queries like leave, salary, and policies.

## 10. Government Service Assistance
**Problem:** Citizens need guidance for government services.  
**Solution:** Chatbots provide step-by-step assistance for applications and services.

## 11. University Admission Enquiries
**Problem:** Students repeatedly ask about admissions.  
**Solution:** Chatbots provide details on courses, eligibility, and deadlines.

## 12. Ticket Booking Assistance
**Problem:** Users need help booking tickets online.  
**Solution:** Chatbots guide seat selection, payment, and confirmation.

## 13. Insurance Claim Support
**Problem:** Customers find claim processes confusing.  
**Solution:** Chatbots explain procedures and collect required documents.
